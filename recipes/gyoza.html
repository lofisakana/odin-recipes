Gyoza Recipe

Description

This recipe teaches how to make gyoza, a type of dumpling very popular in Japan.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cups chopped cabbage
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • ¼ cup chopped carrot
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • ½ pound ground pork
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 (10 ounce) package wonton wrappers
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • ¼ cup water
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
